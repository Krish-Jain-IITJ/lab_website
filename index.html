From the Director

Where Rigorous Theory
Meets Real-World Impact

"Our mission is to develop principled solutions for the control and coordination of complex, networked systems ‚Äî enabling the next generation of intelligent infrastructure."

The Distributed Control Systems Laboratory was established with a vision to address the most pressing challenges at the intersection of control theory, computation, and networked systems. Our work spans rigorous mathematical foundations to hardware-in-loop experiments.


We collaborate closely with industry and government agencies to ensure our research addresses problems of national importance ‚Äî including smart energy grids, autonomous vehicles, and resilient cyber-physical infrastructure.
